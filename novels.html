Novels

Tennessee Yankee




Herb Hughes worked in the high tech industry for more than two decades before leaving to start his own business. This new endeavor was built from the ground up, where he turned something that had not existed before into a household name in the locality. Once the business was thriving, he sold it to pursue his lifelong dream of writing novels.

The author’s previous publications include four nonfiction books and numerous articles and short stories in national and regional periodicals. But the novels were always there, churning in the background, waiting for their chance to spring forward.
